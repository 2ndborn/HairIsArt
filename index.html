About Me

My name is Kara Francis and I have been in the hair industry for over 2 decades. I am a specialist in the colouring and cutting of all hair textures. Making people look and feel their best is something I take pride in as I strongly believe that everyone has something that makes them uniquely beautiful. My job is to bring to light that which is already there, so they can leave my chair oozing with confidence and strutting to the sound of their own personal theme tune.
